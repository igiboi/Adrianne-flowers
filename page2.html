About us

Adrianne graduated from a dentistry and a journalism before she decided to embark herself in what she called “ the beautiful mission of bringing art and beauty to others through flowers”. Her unique background help her communicate exquisite beauty in each design. Inspired by the stunning flowers of her native Colombia, Adriana brings fine art to each design. Whether you love a look that is classic, modern, romantic, vintage, Adrianne Floral Design is committed to fit your personality and help you express your feelings.
